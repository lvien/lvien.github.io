Dark Park

2021

❮ ❯

Overview

This was developed for the 2021 Global Game Jam. It’s been a long day of babysitting. So naturally, you took the lil stinkers to the William Darque local park. You’ve heard some . . . unfriendly things about the area. Should be fine. Only problem is you fell asleep on a bench watching them run around for ages. You wake up realizing it's pitch dark and after closing hours. Better find the kids before they get hurt, or worse--you lose your job. However, a sense of the unfriendly things at this hour seem to add to your troubles. Explore the Dark Park and find the lost kids!

Genre First-Person, Horror
Engine Unity
Team Size 2
