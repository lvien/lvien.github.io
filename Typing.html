Overview

Have you every wanted to test your typing skills, but are bored and seek a better challenge? Or maybe you've never typed using more than two fingers and want to be a master? Congratulations: you've found the ultimate typing tutorial! Choose from 5 difficulties, and get ready to stretch those fingers! You have 60 seconds to see how many wacky, ridiculous prompts you can type before time runs out. Type each prompt quickly before it hits the bottom of the window. A live HUD keeps track of your stats. Remember your home keys, and good luck!
