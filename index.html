I'm Andrew, and I craft games!

I'm a diligent game designer with a strong background in team collaboration, computer science, writing, and communication. I have worked as a gameplay programmer, level designer, modder, and artist for a diverse number of games.

I love creating games that positively motivate players through fun and challenge. The power to overcome obstacles in life – to not be afraid of failure and starting over – is essential for personal growth and learning. And since people naturally love to have fun, why not encourage them to improve themselves through games and technology!

My current mission is to work with other talented fellow developers to create rich, exciting adventures for players and the gaming industry.
