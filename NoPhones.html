No Phones In Class

2019

❮ ❯

Overview

Nothing more boring than waiting for class to end. The only way out is to mess around on your phone, but beware. The teacher will be displeased if he sees you treating his lecture like a game. Whether its answering text messages or googling nonsensical things, do whatever you can to avoid getting caught and escape the endless class!
