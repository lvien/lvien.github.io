Overview

You suddenly wake up and find yourself in the room of a dark, eerie house. Armed only with a gun and your wits, navigate through the pitch-black hallways and find the exit. It won't be easy though - the exits are locked, and there are strange things lurking in the darkness who would love to devour your soul. Find the keys, ammo, and crucifixes to survive and make it out with your life!
